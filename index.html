About Me

I’m a software engineer with a passion for building meaningful, user-centered technology, especially in healthcare, education, and creative wellness. From developing a mobile app that captures real-time nasometry data for speech therapy to creating immersive platforms like Lo-Fi Haven for mental wellness, I thrive at the intersection of innovation, empathy, and design.

Whether leading technical development or collaborating through Figma prototypes, I’m always focused on crafting thoughtful digital experiences that make a positive impact.

Beyond tech, I love music, environmental advocacy, and exploring how technology can empower communities. I'm always curious, always learning, and excited to keep building tools that matter.
